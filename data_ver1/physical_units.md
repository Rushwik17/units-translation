### Metric system

Length: metre (m), kilometre (km), centimetre (cm), millimetre (mm),
micrometre (µm), nanometre (nm)

Mass: kilogram (kg), gram (g), milligram (mg), microgram (µg)

Time: second (s), millisecond (ms), microsecond (µs), nanosecond (ns),
minute (min), hour (h), day (d)

Temperature: kelvin (K), degree Celsius (°C)

Electric current: ampere (A), milliampere (mA), microampere (µA)

Amount of substance: mole (mol), millimole (mmol), micromole (µmol)

Luminous Intensity: candela (cd)

Area: square metre ($m^2$), square centimetre ($cm^2$),
square millimetre ($mm^2$), hectare (ha)

Volume: cubic metre ($m^3$), litre (L), millilitre (mL),
cubic centimetre ($cm^3$)

Speed/velocity: metre per second (m/s), kilometre per hour (km/h)

Acceleration: metre per second squared (m/$s^2$)

Force: newton (N), kilonewton (kN)

Pressure: pascal (Pa), kilopascal (kPa), megapascal (MPa),
bar

Energy/work: joule (J), kilojoule (kJ), megajoule (MJ),
calorie (cal), kilocalorie (kcal)

Power: watt (W), kilowatt (kW), megawatt (MW)

Frequency: hertz (Hz), kilohertz (kHz), megahertz (MHz),
gigahertz (GHz)

Electric charge: coulomb (C)

Voltage: volt (V), millivolt (mV), kilovolt (kV)

Electrical resistance: ohm (Ω), kilo ohm (kΩ), mega-ohm (MΩ)

Electrical conductance: siemens (S)

Capacitance: farad (F), microfarad (µF), nanofarad (nF),
picofarad (pF)

Inductance: henry (H), millihenry (mH), microhenry (µH)

Magnetic flux: weber (Wb)

Magnetic flux density: tesla (T)

Luminous flux: lumen (lm)

Illuminance: lux (lx)

Radioactivity: becquerel (Bq)

Absorbed dose: gray (Gy)

Dose equivalent: sievert (Sv)

Catalytic activity: katal (kat)

### Imperial system

Length: inch (in), foot (ft), yard (yd), mile (mi)

Area: square inch (in^2), square foot (ft^2),
square yard (yd^2), acre, square mile (mi^2)

Volume: fluid ounce (fl oz), pint (pt), quart (qt), gallon (gal)

Mass: ounce (oz), pound (lb), stone,
short ton (US ton), long ton (Imperial ton)

Force: pound-force (lbf)

Pressure: pound per square inch (psi),
inch of mercury (inHg),
foot of water (ftH2O)

Energy/work: foot-pound (ft·lb),
British thermal unit (BTU)

Power: horsepower (hp)

Temperature: degree Fahrenheit (°F),
degree Rankine (°R)

Speed/velocity: miles per hour (mph),
feet per second (ft/s)

Acceleration: feet per second squared (ft/$s^2$)

Density: pound per cubic foot (lb/$ft^3$),
pound per cubic inch (lb/$in^3$)

Flow rate: cubic feet per second (cfs),
gallons per minute (gpm)

Torque: pound-foot (lb·ft),
pound-inch (lb·in)

Energy per unit mass: British thermal unit per pound (BTU/lb)

Heat flux: British thermal unit per square foot (BTU/$ft^2$)

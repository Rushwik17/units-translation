ton:
- US short ton = 2000 pounds (mass)
- Imperial long ton = 2240 pounds (mass)
- Metric tonne = 1000 kilograms (mass)

pound / pounds:
- pound (lb) = unit of mass/weight
- pound (£) = currency (British pound sterling)

ounce / ounces:
- ounce (oz) = unit of mass
- fluid ounce (fl oz) = unit of volume

stone:
- unit of mass (14 pounds)
- colloquial term meaning “a rock” (non-measurement)

yard:
- unit of length
- outdoor area around a house (non-measurement)

degree:
- unit of angle
- unit of temperature (°C, °F)

second:
- unit of time
- ordinal number (2nd)

minute:
- unit of time
- adjective meaning very small (“minute detail”)

day:
- unit of time
- daytime period (non-measurement)
